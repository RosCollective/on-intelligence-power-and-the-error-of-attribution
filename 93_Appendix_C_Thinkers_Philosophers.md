Thinkers and Philosophers

Chronological list by birth year with geography 
Format: Name (birth–death) | Field | Geography | Contextual harm

Laozi (601 BCE–531 BCE)
Field: Philosopher
Geography: Ancient China
Contextual harm: Teachings institutionalized and stripped of dissenting philosophical stance

Field: Philosopher, educator
Geography: Lu State, Ancient China
Contextual harm: Political exile and lifelong marginalization from power and influence

Socrates (470 BCE–399 BCE)
Field: Philosopher
Geography: Athens, Ancient Greece
Contextual harm: Executed by the state for corrupting youth and impiety

Mozi (470 BCE–391 BCE)
Field: Philosopher
Geography: Warring States China

Geography: Persia

Averroes (Ibn Rushd) (1126–1198)

Geography: Al-Andalus

Immanuel Kant (1724–1804)
